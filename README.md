# InQik

**Google Business Profile Security + Management for Local Businesses**

InQik is a managed service company that handles Google Business Profile optimization, review response, local rank tracking, and profile protection for local businesses worldwide. We serve restaurants, dental clinics, salons, law firms, fitness studios, real estate agencies, and other local service businesses across the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, and India.

Business owners don't need to learn any software or log into any dashboard. Our team handles everything and delivers monthly reports with live dashboard access.
