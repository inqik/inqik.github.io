Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Do I need to do anything after signing up?
No. After a quick onboarding call where we learn about your business, we handle everything. You get a monthly report and can check your dashboard anytime, but there is nothing you need to do. We manage it all.
What exactly does InQik do?
We make sure your business shows up when customers search on Google. We optimize your Google Business Profile, respond to all reviews, post regularly, track your local rankings, and protect you from fake reviews and competitor attacks.
Can you guarantee I will be #1 on Google?
No one can honestly guarantee rankings. What we guarantee: your profile will be 100% complete, all reviews responded within 48 hours, and Google Posts published regularly. These actions consistently improve visibility over time.
How long until I see results?
Your profile is optimized within 5-7 days. Visibility improvements usually start within 2-3 weeks. Our rank tracking heatmaps show exactly where improvements happen over time so you can see real progress.
What if I already have a Google Business Profile?
That's great. Most businesses use only 30-40% of what Google offers. We audit your existing profile and optimize everything that is missing. The improvement is usually significant and visible within weeks.
What is the difference between Essential and Growth?
Essential handles all profile management: posts, reviews, monitoring, protection, and reporting. Growth adds local rank tracking heatmaps from 49 GPS points, competitor tracking, AI-powered recommendations, and 24/7 profile protection with instant alerts.
What happens if my profile gets attacked?
Our monitoring catches threats immediately. Fake reviews get flagged and reported to Google with evidence. "Permanently closed" edits get reversed. You receive an incident report documenting every event and our response.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No long-term contracts. We ask for 30 days notice. That's it.
